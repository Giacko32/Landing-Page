Technical Details

  • Captivating Display

    The 16-inch display on the ROG Strix G16 (2023) offers an extraordinary visual experience with its WQXGA resolution of 2560 x 1600 pixels. With an ultra-responsive 165Hz refresh rate and a 3ms response time, every movement on the screen is fluid and blur-free, ideal for gamers who don’t want to compromise. Additionally, the anti-glare IPS panel ensures excellent visibility in any lighting condition, while the 100% DCI-P3 color gamut coverage provides vivid and accurate color reproduction, perfect for enjoying the finest details in your favorite games.

  • Top-Notch Processing Power

    At the heart of this notebook is the Intel Core i9-13980HX processor from the 13th generation, a CPU with 24 cores designed to handle even the most demanding workloads. Whether you're gaming on the most intensive titles or working on 3D rendering projects, the ROG Strix G16 delivers extraordinary performance, with a turbo frequency that can reach up to 5.6 GHz. The generous 36MB cache helps reduce waiting times, making every operation swift and uninterrupted.

  • Dreamlike Graphics

    When it comes to graphics, this notebook does not disappoint with the NVIDIA GeForce RTX 4070 card with 8 GB of GDDR6 VRAM. Based on the Ada Lovelace architecture, the RTX 4070 not only offers exceptional performance in all modern games but also supports advanced technologies like ray tracing and DLSS 3, elevating visual quality to new levels. Get ready for an immersive gaming experience with realistic shadows and incredible details that make every virtual world more lifelike than ever.

  • Limitless Memory and Storage

    With 32 GB of DDR5 RAM at 4800 MHz, the ROG Strix G16 is built for extreme multitasking. Effortlessly switch from a gaming session to a video conference, or from video editing to 3D rendering, all with the same fluidity. The 1 TB NVMe PCIe 4.0 SSD not only ensures ultra-fast loading times but also provides ample space to install numerous games, applications, and multimedia files. And if you need more space, you can easily expand the storage thanks to the additional M.2 SSD slot.

  • Intelligent Cooling

    Keep your notebook always running at peak efficiency with ROG Intelligent Cooling technology. This advanced system uses liquid metal compound to improve heat dissipation from the CPU, while the dual Arc Flow fan and heatsink with 4 heat pipes work together to keep temperatures under control even during the most intense gaming sessions. You can also customize the cooling modes via the Armoury Crate software, choosing between Silent, Performance, and Turbo, depending on your needs.

  • Advanced Connectivity for the Modern Gamer

    Staying connected and making the most of your hardware is easy with the ROG Strix G16. The USB Type-C port with DisplayPort 1.4 and Power Delivery support allows you to connect external monitors and charge the device, while the Wi-Fi 6E connection ensures incredible network speeds with low latency. Other ports, including USB 3.2, HDMI 2.1, and Thunderbolt 4, allow you to connect a wide range of peripherals for a complete gaming setup.

  • Long Battery Life and Fast Charging

    Don’t let a cable slow you down: with a 90 Wh battery, the ROG Strix G16 offers up to 8 hours of mixed-use, allowing you to game or work longer without worrying about the charge. And when it’s time to recharge, the fast-charging feature lets you bring the battery back to 50% in just 30 minutes, so you’re always ready to go.

  • Ergonomic and Immersive Design

    The mechanical chiclet keyboard with customizable RGB backlighting via Aura Sync is not only aesthetically pleasing but also designed to offer precise tactile feedback during long gaming sessions. The robust chassis design, with the illuminated ROG logo and RGB light bar under the chassis, gives the notebook a futuristic and eye-catching look, reflecting your passion for gaming. Despite its power, the notebook maintains a slim profile and lightweight build, ideal for gamers who need portability.

  • Immersive Audio and Video

    With support for Dolby Atmos and Hi-Res Audio, the ROG Strix G16 transforms sound into a three-dimensional experience, completely enveloping you in the game. The speakers with Smart Amp Technology provide powerful and clear audio, while the AI noise-canceling microphone ensures crisp communication during multiplayer matches. The integrated 720p webcam, with Windows Hello support, ensures decent video quality for video calls, also offering advanced security features.
